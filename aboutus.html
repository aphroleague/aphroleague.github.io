About Us

Aphro was formed in June 2016. Since then, it's shut down, retuned, shut down, and recently making a comeback in 2017. We're a group of serious battlers who love to socialize and gradually improve our skills. Aphro has an organized hiearchy system, with ranks in order from Initiate, Gym Leader, Ace, Professor, Elite Four and the Champion. Think you got what it takes?
